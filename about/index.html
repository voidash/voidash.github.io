What i strive for

My lifelong pursuit, no matter how time and environment shifts. It’s going to be “self-ownership” or self sovereignty. I bet it on Intersection of Hardware and cryptography. Paraphrasing the quote on a book “Cypherpunks: Freedom and Future of the Internet”

Strong cryptography can resist an unlimited application of violence. No amount of coercive force will ever solve a math problem.

Cryptography is tool to bring the power back to humanity. Cryptography will save the internet. Cryptography is the one ingredient of self ownership. Another is the very hardware that makes storing, transmitting of information possible. My goal is to capitalize on this very notion of self sovereignty.
